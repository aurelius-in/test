### Problem Statement
#### What
What:
- Manual, labor-intensive process for sorting, optimizing, and annotating provider files.
- Risk of errors in manual selection of appropriate files for review.
- High time investment: averaging 30 minutes for optimization and 1 hour for bookmarking each record.

#### Why
- Coordinators processed 359 records last year, with substantial time investment (1795 hours for optimization, 3590 hours for bookmarking).
- Manual handling limits the ability to scale and respond to growing demand.

#### Desired Outcome
- Automated system for gathering and preparing records for optimization and bookmarking.
- Significant reduction in time and effort required by coordinators, leading to improved efficiency.

### Deliver Value
#### What
- AI/ML tools to optimize the selection and preparation of records automatically.
- A system for intelligent bookmarking based on relevance and date, improving record accuracy and accessibility.
- Pattern identification to predict outlier triggers and abusive provider behavior.

#### Why
- Automation will streamline the process, enabling coordinators to handle a larger volume of records.
- Intelligent systems can improve accuracy, reduce errors, and save thousands of hours annually.

#### Desired Outcome
- A fully automated process that reduces the time spent per record from hours to minutes.
- An intelligent system that learns and improves over time, increasing efficiency and reliability.

### Solution & Integration
#### What
- Development of a tool that selects Member records from a shared folder and prepares them for optimization.
- Implementation of Machine Learning to identify patterns and help organize bookmarks effectively.
- Creation of a system that filters and appends bookmarks based on custom criteria such as date and relevance.

#### Why
- To leverage technology to perform tasks that are currently manual and labor-intensive.
- To ensure that the right information is available to the right people at the right time.

#### Desired Outcome
- A seamless process that enables coordinators to focus on more complex tasks.
- A scalable solution that can adapt to increasing data volumes and complexity.
