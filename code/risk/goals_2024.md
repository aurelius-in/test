### Primary goals and initiatives for 2024, Key areas and quarterly objectives:

1. **Improve Digital NPS:**
   - Goals include enhancing the provider search experience, claim submission, and viewing process to improve Net Promoter Scores (NPS).
   - Initiatives involve migrating to new platforms, redesigning user interfaces, and increasing digital engagement.

2. **Provider Search and Claims Submission:**
   - Objectives include improving the accessibility of claims submission and streamlining the view claim process.
   - The purpose is to provide parity with off-cycle provider search experiences and to ensure timely and efficient claim submissions.

3. **Digital Adoption:**
   - Aims to decrease support call volume due to login issues and increase member registration on the LAWW platform.
   - Plans to integrate with an enterprise GitHub program to boost developer productivity.

4. **Percentage of Members Guided toward Self-Care:**
   - Focuses on improving self-care navigation and quality of Care Explorer recommendations.
   - Efforts are directed towards personalizing experiences and improving the specificity of recommendations.

5. **EWS (Various Products and Systems):**
   - Seeks to improve user experience with contact centers, enhance mental health capabilities, and integrate with Salesforce.
   - Efforts include vendor integration to prioritize against AW product capital and improvements in operational efficiency for EAP specialists.

6. **Global Tech Application Management and Network Tiering:**
   - Aims to replace offshore telephony platforms with more efficient systems.
   - Strategies include expanding network tiering and improving provider network tiering with clear ownership and maintenance protocols.

Each initiative is designed to enhance user experiences, improve operational efficiency, and increase the effectiveness of provider analysis and member engagement tools. The overarching goal is to leverage technology to streamline processes, enhance data-driven decision-making, and support better health outcomes.
