### Statement of Professional Goals

#### Praxis Research Interests and Rationale
##### Problem Motivation
The central motivation for my praxis research comes from the critical need to improve mental healthcare through innovative technological solutions. Mental health conditions often go unnoticed until they become severe, creating a significant gap in care that can be addressed through the application of AI technologies. By leveraging AI, we can transform mental healthcare, making it more proactive, personalized, and efficient.

##### Problem Statement/Hypothesis
I propose to develop an integrated AI system that facilitates early detection and provides immediate support for mental health issues. This system will use predictive analytics and interactive AI tools to identify early signs of mental health conditions and offer timely interventions.

##### Project Purpose
The project's primary goal is to deliver a proactive mental healthcare approach. By employing AI for early detection and support, the project aims to significantly enhance patient care, reduce the long-term treatment costs, and improve overall mental health outcomes. The integration of AI into mental healthcare can revolutionize the field, offering new avenues for patient care and support.

##### Project Plan Goals
- To develop algorithms for early detection of mental health conditions using patient data.
- To create AI-driven chatbots that provide real-time, empathetic support to individuals seeking mental health assistance.
- To analyze social media and other data sources for broader mental health trend analysis, contributing to a more comprehensive understanding of mental health needs and challenges.
