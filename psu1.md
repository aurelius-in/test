# Statement of Professional Goals

## Interest in the Program and Professional Goals

The Doctor of Engineering program at Penn State uniquely blends advanced engineering with leadership principles, aligning with my aspirations to lead in the healthcare technology sector. Penn State’s reputation for top-tier engineering education, combined with its focus on real-world applications, makes it an ideal setting for my professional growth. This program's structure, transitioning from academic research to industrial and commercial leadership, perfectly complements my ambition to be at the forefront of healthcare innovation, driving change through cutting-edge technology.

The practical orientation of the program resonates deeply with my desire to transform healthcare through AI and ML technologies. It provides an unparalleled opportunity to develop new products and processes that have tangible impacts in various industries, especially healthcare. By engaging in this program, I aim to refine my skills in synthesizing critical information and leading high-performing teams, ultimately contributing to breakthroughs in healthcare technology.

## Academic and Professional Preparation

My academic journey, culminating in a Master's degree in Software Development/Engineering from Boston University, has laid a solid foundation in machine learning and AI. This educational background, coupled with rigorous research in drone surveillance using novel ML approaches, has prepared me for advanced studies in engineering. My academic achievements reflect a deep understanding of the theoretical aspects of AI and its practical applications.

In the professional realm, my experiences are diverse and impactful. Leading AI initiatives at Optum UHG, I have developed solutions that significantly improve healthcare outcomes. At the Naval Surface Warfare Center, my work in defense technology involved pioneering applications of AI in critical national security projects. My military service as a Combat Medic in the US Army instilled in me a strong sense of discipline and the ability to thrive under pressure. These experiences collectively have equipped me with a unique blend of technical expertise and leadership skills, essential for the D.Eng. program.

## Praxis Research Overview

The flexibility of the D.Eng. program at Penn State is particularly appealing as it allows for both discipline-specific and cross-disciplinary research. My proposed praxis research will focus on applying advanced neural networks and transformers in mental health care applications. This area of research is not only technologically challenging but also has the potential to bring significant improvements in patient care and treatment outcomes.

At Optum UHG, I have access to a wealth of resources and a supportive environment for conducting this applied research. My role allows me to leverage state-of-the-art technologies and methodologies, which will be instrumental in my research. The opportunity to undertake this research at Penn State, backed by the resources at my disposal at Optum UHG, positions me to contribute meaningful and innovative solutions to the healthcare industry.

## Conclusion

Embarking on the Doctor of Engineering program at Penn State represents a significant milestone in my professional journey. This program is a gateway to not just advancing my knowledge but also to becoming a leader who can effectively bridge technology with practical applications, especially in healthcare. My commitment is to leverage this opportunity to its fullest potential, contributing to the field of healthcare technology through innovative research and leadership.
